# Connection Graph — Freeman Community

## How to read
```
@user1 --[relationship type]--> @user2
```

Relationship types:
- **colleague** — work together on the team
- **frequent** — interact often in chats
- **mentions** — one mentions the other
- **project** — collaborate on a project
- **responds** — one responds to the other more than usual
- **debugging** — solved technical problems together
